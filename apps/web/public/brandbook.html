00 / Positioning

Built for non-technical businesses with real workflow pain.

AgentsOX should not sound like a generic AI agency. The brand is for business owners, clinics, local service operators, real-estate offices, coaches, and e-commerce teams that need custom systems around existing operations.

Positioning statement

For business owners dealing with legacy systems, missed leads, manual admin, unclear branding, weak SEO, and messy workflows, AgentsOX is the custom AI automation partner that listens first, ships fast, and keeps improving the system until the client feels safe using it.

10-second belief

AgentsOX can understand my business problem and build a practical system that solves it.

Case-study belief

AgentsOX executes, integrates, and supports real business workflows, not just AI demos.

Primary buyers

  • Clinics and MedSpas
  • Local service operators
  • Real-estate offices
  • Coaches and appointment-based experts
  • E-commerce stores

Brand posture

Technical and premium, warm and founder-led, quiet and enterprise-ready. Serious without becoming cold.

Not this

No AI-bro hype, no mystical language, no generic platform claims, no corporate consulting fog, and no developer-only language.

02 / Trust architecture

Stay until the client feels safe.

The core trust signal is not "AI expertise" by itself. It is listening, translating the client's problem, building a working system, explaining it clearly, and handling the failure paths.

Trust promise

We listen, build around your real workflow, explain the system clearly, and keep improving it until you trust it.

Proof types
  • Client work and screenshots
  • Short demos
  • Automations built
  • Before/after workflow maps
  • Technical case studies

AI reliability

Show human checkpoints, fallback paths, monitoring, and what happens when a model or API fails.

Data privacy

Document what data is touched, where it flows, what is stored, and what is intentionally not retained.

Vendor lock-in

Prefer clear handoff, understandable systems, and maintainable tools over unnecessary proprietary dependency.

03 / Logo system

Ox strength, agent intelligence, precision engineering.

The logo system keeps the old OX equity but makes it simpler and more implementation-safe. Use the SVG mark and lockup for production. Use the generated PNG as a concept reference, not as the only source of truth.

AgentsOX

Minimum sizes

Primary with descriptor: 360px digital / 55mm print. Primary without descriptor: 220px / 35mm. Icon-only: 24px UI, 16px favicon.

Clear space

Use the O height in OX as X. Keep 0.5X around the lockup and 25% icon width around the standalone mark.

Do not

Stretch, rotate, recolor randomly, add heavy glow, place over busy photos, or rebuild the wordmark in unrelated fonts.

06 / Visual and UI style

Operational software, not decorative AI theater.

Interfaces should feel dense but controlled. Use real workflow screenshots, monitoring loops, automation states, and implementation proof. Avoid generic robot imagery and purple neon stock visuals.

Iconography

Outlined, geometric icons with rounded caps. Use 2px strokes at 24px. Keep icons simple enough to match the ox mark.

Radii

Buttons: 8-12px. Cards and panels: 8px. Large containers: 12px. Pills: 999px only for true tags.

Spacing

Use a 4px base scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96. Keep content max width near 1126px.

07 / Voice and verbal identity

Direct, technical, calm, accountable.

The brand speaks like a builder who can ship and stay accountable. It should be clear enough for non-technical buyers and precise enough to earn trust.

Direct

Say what the system does. Use verbs like build, route, monitor, recover, schedule, and ship.

Engineer-focused

Use proof, constraints, tools, and reliability language when it helps people trust the implementation.

Calm authority

No hype, no magic, no unsupported autonomy claims. Keep human control explicit.

Warm accountability

Explain clearly, stay close to the client, and make the workflow feel safe before calling the project done.
