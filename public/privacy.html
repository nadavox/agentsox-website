  • Business name: AgentsOX
  • Website: agentsox.com
  • Contact email: atlas@agentsox.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with our services:

  • Messages: Content you send to us via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email, including text, images, and attachments.
  • Contact information: Your phone number, name, and email address as provided by the messaging platform or by you directly.
  • Usage data: Timestamps, message delivery status, and interaction logs necessary for service operation.
  • Website data: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited). We do not use cookies for tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate our automated business services (scheduling, booking, communication management).
  • Respond to your messages, requests, and inquiries.
  • Improve our services, diagnose issues, and maintain security.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

4. Automated Processing & AI Disclosure

Our services use artificial intelligence (AI) and large language models (LLMs) to process your messages and generate responses. This means:

  • Your message content is sent to AI service providers (via OpenRouter) for processing.
  • We do not share your name, phone number, or other personal identifiers with AI providers — only the message content necessary to generate a response.
  • AI-generated responses are automated. A human operator may review conversations for quality and safety purposes.

5. WhatsApp Messaging

If you communicate with us via WhatsApp:

  • We use the official WhatsApp Cloud API provided by Meta to send and receive messages.
  • By sending us a message on WhatsApp, you consent to receiving automated replies from our business.
  • You can opt out at any time by sending "STOP" or by blocking our number. We will cease messaging you promptly.
  • Information obtained from WhatsApp about you is used only to support our messaging conversation with you, as required by WhatsApp Business Policy.
  • Messages are encrypted in transit using the Signal protocol as provided by WhatsApp.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Cloud API: Message delivery. WhatsApp Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram Bot API: Message delivery. Telegram Privacy Policy.
  • OpenRouter: AI/LLM processing for generating responses. Message content only (no personal identifiers).
  • Google Workspace: Calendar and email functionality.
  • Cloudflare: Website hosting and DNS. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Zoho Mail: Business email hosting. Zoho Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

  • Message data and interaction logs are retained for up to 90 days for service operation and debugging.
  • After 90 days, message content is deleted. Basic contact records may be retained longer for service continuity.
  • You may request immediate deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) for all data in transit.
  • Access controls and authentication on all systems.
  • Secure, hardened server infrastructure with regular security updates.
  • Encrypted backups stored in isolated, off-site storage.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt out: Stop receiving messages by sending "STOP" on WhatsApp or Telegram, or by unsubscribing from email.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Complaint: Contact us with any privacy concerns.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at atlas@agentsox.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
